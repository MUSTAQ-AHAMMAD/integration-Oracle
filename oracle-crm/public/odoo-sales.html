🏦 Receipt Method & Bank Account IDs (FusionReceiptMethod / VendhqRegisters fields)

Used by FusionStdReceiptMapping and FusionMiscReceiptMapping in the middleware. The Receipt Method ID is the Oracle AR receipt method; the Bank Account ID is the remittance bank account (getCashAccountId() / getBankAccountId()).

FusionReceiptMethod.getReceiptMethodId() — applies to the default payment type above
FusionMiscReceiptMapping: ReceiptMethodName on miscellaneous receipts
FusionReceiptMethod.getReceiptIsCash() — When checked, uses Cash Account ID instead of Bank Account ID for RemittanceBankAccountId (FusionStdReceiptMapping)
VendhqRegisters.getBankAccountId() — RemittanceBankAccountId for non-cash payments
VendhqRegisters.getCashAccountId() — RemittanceBankAccountId for cash payments
VendhqRegisters.getBankAccount() — RemittanceBankAccountName on miscellaneous receipts
FusionSalesMetadata.getRecActivityNameBank() — ReceivableActivityName on misc receipts
